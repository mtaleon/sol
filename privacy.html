Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

This app does not collect personal data directly. Advertising data is handled by Google AdMob.

Sudoku by Octile is a puzzle game that respects your privacy. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, except as described below for third-party advertising services.

Data Storage

All game data — your progress, settings, and statistics — is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service operated by Google, to display ads. AdMob independently collects and processes certain data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • App interaction data for ad performance measurement

This data is collected and handled by Google as an independent data controller. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy > Advertising on most devices).

No Accounts

This app does not require or support user accounts. There is no login, no registration, and no personal information collected by us.

No Analytics

Apart from AdMob, this app does not use any additional analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into this app is Google AdMob for advertising. The app also contains an optional link to Octile, shown after completing a puzzle, which navigates to an external page if tapped.

Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Ad requests are not configured with child-directed treatment tags. If you believe a child has provided personal data through a third-party service while using this app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to octileapp@googlegroups.com.
