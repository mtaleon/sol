How to Play

Fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with the digits 1–9. Each digit appears exactly once in each region.

Controls

  • Tap a cell to select it, then tap a number to place it.
  • Use Notes to mark possible candidates in empty cells.
  • Undo reverses your last action. Long-press Undo to redo.
  • Clear removes the value. Long-press Clear to erase notes.
  • Hint fills the selected cell with the correct value.
  • Pause the game anytime from the header. Your progress is auto-saved.
